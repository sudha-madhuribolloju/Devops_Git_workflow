1.Define Devops?
A:DevOps is a software development approach and cultural philosophy that brings software
development (Dev) and IT operations (Ops) together to work collaboratively throughout the
entire software lifecycle — from planning and coding to testing, deployment and maintenance.
Its aim is to deliver applications and services faster, more reliably, and with higher quality
than traditional siloed methods.
2. Define AWS?
A: AWS (Amazon Web Services) is a comprehensive cloud computing platform provided by Amazon
that delivers a wide range of on-demand services over the Internet — including computing power,
storage, databases, networking, analytics, machine learning, and more — on a pay-as-you-go
basis.
